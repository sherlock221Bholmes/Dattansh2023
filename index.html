WELCOME TO DATTANSH

Symbiosis Centre for Information Technology (SCIT) organizes an Annual Analytics Conclave every year, named DATTANSH, for all trending and upcoming technologies & applications in the world of Data Analytics. Dattansh provides a forum for participants to gain an expert perspective on the exciting world of data analytics by competing in various contests, and to learn about the journey of data in real-life and industrial use cases by connecting with knowledgeable speakers. Come and join us in this journey to the space of data and experience extravagance in terms of knowledge sharing, and competition. The 5th edition to be held on 21 January 2023.

Datafication: Quantifying the Unimaginable

Datafication encompasses the collective tools, technologies and processes used to transform an organization into a data-driven enterprise. The brilliance of datafication lies in its ability to capture one's thoughts, actions and activities into a numerically quantified format called data. This data can then be cleaned, modelled and analyzed to generate insights that can not only change but potentially disrupt the existing nature of industries. Often confused with "digitisation", this phenomenon has a far broader horizon and is helping us understand the world in a way which was never done before.

Data Yuga: The Era Where Data Dictates

The modern world revolves around information and this data is vital to make all business and technological decisions. The current era is the age of data or the “Data Yuga”; in which everything is about data. It has sprouted the fields of Data Science and Data Analytics, both of which inspire innovation and open-minded thinking. Insights from data can be used to make informed decisions and build powerful systems and technologies like Smart robots, autonomous vehicles and artificial intelligence. Generations of knowledge have been stored in ancient texts such as the Vedas and in ancient relics which are still waiting to be deciphered and the knowledge within them hidden for the moment. Data Yuga has the potential to offer solutions which reduce computation times using quantum computing and artificial intelligence, empowering us to evolve even further.

Data Odyssey: Voyage through Data

A huge amount of data gets generated at an alarming rate, daily, on every reach of what we do. With the understanding and proper utilization, this data offers an opportunity to make decisions solely based on information, not the emotions, and to analyze the previous happenings or situations for an accurate outcome. The ability to channelize this data and use it for the advancement of our lives is at our fingertips. While questing for the best practices and results, data goes through multiple processes like extraction, preparation, analysis and interpretation. "Data Odyssey" is about exploring this venture through the experience. Dattansh provides a platform to have an expert outlook into the fascinating world of data analytics by participating in various competitions and ultimately know about the journey of data in real life and industrial use cases by interacting with adept speakers.

Data Nova: Transcend through Data

The amount of data that is currently collected and processed is perhaps the most spectacular result of the digital revolution. There has never been such an information explosion in human history. Smartphones, sensors, and connected vehicles and appliances, among other digital artefacts, are the reason behind this data explosion. Much of what we touch and work with now creates data automatically.The data boom over the last ten years has generated a virtuous cycle of data analysis and action, resulting in new insights. As firms scramble to modernise their businesses and make data-driven decisions, we've seen them acquire more data than ever before. Data Nova will take you to a virtual world where data and innovation converge to create an explosion of problem-solving, analytics and excitement.
